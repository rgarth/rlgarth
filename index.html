The Extraordinary Penelope Lemon

Middle Grade

Ten-year-old Penelope Lemon discovers that the overgrown corner of her backyard is home to something impossible—fairies. Real ones. And they're at war. Now Penelope must navigate ancient grudges, tiny politics, and her own doubts to bring peace to her garden.

A story about seeing the magic that was always there.

Bone Eaters

Young Adult

You were told tooth fairies leave coins under pillows. You weren't told what they really are. For centuries, an ancient curse kept the Bone Eaters bound—tiny unseelie fae who could only consume teeth that fell on their own. Now the curse is broken, and they hunger for more. When the Bone Eaters take his sister, Fergus will do whatever it takes to get her back.

A dark fantasy about the true origin of tooth fairies.
